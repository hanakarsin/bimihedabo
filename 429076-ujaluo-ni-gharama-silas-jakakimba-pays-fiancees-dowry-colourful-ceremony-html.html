City lawyer Silas Chepkeres Jakakimba formalised his union to fiancée on Saturday, October 9.

Jakakimba paid his Flo's dowry in a colourful event held in Migori County. The flashy Luo man wore a dark blue custom made suit while Flo wore a long body-hugging maroon dress, and she looked gorgeous.

Jakakimba off the market

The lawyer turned politician proposed to his longtime girlfriend Florene, aka Nyasuba, on August 31, 2021.

Jakakimba went down on one knee and asked Flo to marry him in a beautiful setting resembling a hotel room.

The room was sprinkled with red rose petals the same colour as Flo's dress on that day.

His proposal came a few weeks after he was involved in a drama with the mother of his two kids.
