Davido Replies Man Who Embarked On Road Trip From Benue To Lagos Just To See Him

April 04, 2024
Post

Nigerian singer, David Adeleke popularly known as Davido have responded to a Benue-based fan who embarked on a road trip just to meet him.

This is coming after a Twitter influencer, SSbmuzik called the singer’s attention to his fan’s notable quest.

According to SSBmuzik, Emmiwuks is currently on day eight of his trip.

He wrote: “A young man called @emmiwuks embarked on a Benue to Lagos trip on bicycle to meet Afrobeat super @davido. He’s currently on Day 8.”

In response to the post, the singer stressed that he’s currently not at home. He then urged the fan to turn around.

Davido Replied: “Turn around I am not home.”
