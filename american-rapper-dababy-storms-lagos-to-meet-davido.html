American Rapper, DaBaby Storms Lagos To Meet Davido

March 08, 2024
Post

American rapper, Jonathan Lyndale Kirk popularly known as DaBaby, has stormed the city of Lagos, Nigeria.

The musician was hosted by Nigerian musician and philanthropist, David Adeleke better known as Davido.

Naija News understands that DaBaby came all the way from Germany to do a music video with Davido.

Davido gave the visitor a warm welcome and promised take him through a tour around Lagos city.

DaBaby has since taken to his social media page, Instagram precisely to announce his arrival in Nigeria.

First, he visited a shoe store where he bought a few sneakers, then, he shared a video of himself among a crowd of Lagosians, before entering his vehicle.

Also, he sprayed some amount of dollars to the crowd whereafter a man from the crowd warned him to hold his phone tight.

Still cruising around, the musician shared a video of him with Davido in his Lamborghini, DaBaby asked “where are we going” and Davido replied, saying “tonight, I will show you Lagos”.

Announcing DaBaby’s arrival earlier, Davido wrote: “My boy finna touch down the Motherland.”

Meanwhile, a video of Isreal DMW, an aide to Davido, sharing money to some young men has surfaced online.

In the now viral video, Isreal is seen handing out some naira notes to some men, security officials included.

Nigerians have praised Isreal for his kindness, insisting that he is following in the footstep of his boss, Davido.
