Jude Okoye Tweets Alarming Message On Possible P-Square Breakup

May 13, 2024
Post

Confusion and anxiety have been trailing rumours and insinuations that popular music duo, Peter and Paul of PSquare, may be splitting up after all these years.

These fears from fans have been heightened recently by an alarming tweet which the groups older brother, Jude Engees Okoye, made today. The message is quite vague, but seems to point a lot at the possibility of a break up between the twin brothers.

Despite the tweet, Jude’s Twitter bio remains- “President/C.E.O Square Records, Northside Music Inc and Northside Entertainment Ltd. PSquare’s Manager/Producer Director, also pointing to the fact that the group is still intact.

But then there are reports the tweet may not be unconnected to the alleged recent fights between Paul and Peter Okoye.

Peter, Jude Engees and Paul Okoye

Speculations are rife that the twins now fight over everything including songs, videos and ideas.Other reports claim that Jude is tired of wadding in, settling the constants spats or even sometimes being a party in the vicious arguments; hence his tweet.

But then, despite evidence of Jude’s tweet, the Public Relations Executive to Psquare, Bayo Adetu, has decribed the story of Jude dumping his brothers as untrue.

He said…“I am too busy to give some wanna be blogger traffic, I don’t know where that report is coming from. Maybe they just want to get traffic to their site. It’s baseless and untrue”.

"Nonetheless, we hear that family members, friends and industry professionals are racing to clarify issues with Jude with the aim of banishing such a thought, should it be true!

The twin brothers have been a team in the music industry for 10 years now, bagging various awards and growing even more popular with time. There is no doubt that a split would break the heart of millions of fans all over the world.
