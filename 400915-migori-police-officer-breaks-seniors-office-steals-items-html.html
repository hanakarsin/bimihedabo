A junior police officer has become a subject in the ongoing investigations after he allegedly stole some items from his senior's office in Migori county

Police constable Nick Kimathi Ndegwa was said to have broken into the office of the officer commanding Macalder police station (OCS) from where he made away with undisclosed items.

A report which was filed at Macalder police station in Nyatike sub-county showed Kimathi allegedly committed the said offense on Monday, January 25.
