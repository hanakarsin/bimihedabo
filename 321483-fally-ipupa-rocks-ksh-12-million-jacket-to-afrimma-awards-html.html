Congolese musician Fally Ipupa is undoubtedly one of the richest musicians in Africa.

This, the Eloko Oyo crooner, has proven countless times with his lifestyle and how he rolls, be it in his home country or abroad on tours.

Ipupa recently left jaws on the floor as he stepped out wearing a very expensive piece of fashion.

The star on Friday, October 25, joined hundreds of celebrities and their fans at the Lofty Spaces in Dallas, for the AFRIMMA 2019.

Like a real celebrity, Ipupa dressed to kill.

He attended the awards gala rocking a Louis Vuiton half jacket estimated at KSh 1.2 million.

Clearly, the star attended the event knowing he will be bringing the house down as he also won an award as best male singer in Central Africa.

His fashion statement impressed quite a number of people who duly praised him for being an ardent fashion enthusiast.

Among those who were impressed by Fally's expensive half coat was Kenyan media personality Willy M Tuva who got the opportunity to even touch it.

''How much would you spend on a jacket? Well, Congolese singer Fally Ipupa spent an estimated amount of over KSh 1.2 Million on the jacket he wore during the Afrimma 2019 in Dallas, Texas, where he also won the Best Male Central Africa award. What would you do with $ 12, 258?" Tuva wrote on Twitter.

This, however, did not come as a surprise to many since Ipupa is considered one of the many fashion savvy celebrities in the world.
