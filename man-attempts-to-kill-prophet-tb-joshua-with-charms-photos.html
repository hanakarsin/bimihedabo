Man Attempts To 'Kill' Prophet TB Joshua With Charms (Photos)

July 16, 2024
Post

Prophet TB Joshua has alleged that a young man, whose name was not mentioned, came to the Synagogue Church of All Nations (SCOAN) in Lagos to attack him with charms.

Naija News reports that the clergyman made the allegation on the micro-blogging site in a tweet on his twitter handle.

Prophet Joshua said the man came to the SCOAN with charms meant to kill and destroy and that he usually makes incantations and calls the name of his victims and they would die.

Prophet Joshua said through his twitter handle, said when he was approaching the man, the young man removed the charms to attack him but the Holy Spirit rendered the charms impotent.

The tweet reads: “This young man came to The SCOAN with charms meant to kill and destroy.

“He would make incantations and call the name of his victims and they would die.

“When the man of God was approaching him, he removed the charms to attack him but the Holy Spirit rendered the charms impotent.”
