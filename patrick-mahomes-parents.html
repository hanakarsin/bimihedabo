Who are Patrick Mahomes's parents?

Patrick Mahomes II was born on Sept. 17, 1995, to parents Pat Mahomes and Randi Martin. His father is Black and his mother is white, making him biracial. The couple reportedly divorced when Patrick was just 11 years old — but for the sake of their son, they've remained civil and are able to watch his football games amicably.

Patrick's father, Pat Mahomes Sr., was a professional athlete as well. He is a former MLB pitcher who played for quite a few teams, including the Minnesota Twins, Boston Red Sox, New York Mets, Texas Rangers, Chicago Cubs, and the Pittsburgh Pirates, from 1992 until 2003.

Well, there you have it: Patrick was certainly born with athletic genes and grew up around professional sports, thanks to his dad. Although he didn't choose to take the pro baseball route, Patrick did play baseball at Texas Tech University for a few years. However, he opted to pursue a professional football career and was drafted by the Chiefs in 2017.

Does Patrick Mahomes have siblings?

Patrick's mother, Randi, just like his father, is also very proud of her successful son. She is also a mom to Patrick's two younger siblings, brother Jackson Mahomes and sister Mia Randall, both of whom Patrick adores.

Patrick's parents and his siblings are often featured on the professional athlete's Instagram page with heartwarming posts. By the looks of it, despite his parents' divorce, they are still a tight-knit, supportive family.

In a 2018 interview with KSHB-TV, Randi gushed about how Patrick is truly living the dream.

"It’s truly been a blessing," she explained. "I tell him that all the time. [It's] a blessing that, you know, little boys dream about. He's gotten to actually live a dream. So, you know, we take it — I take it one day at a time and try to enjoy it. It is a little overwhelming."

Is Patrick Mahomes married? Does he have kids?

Patrick has built his own adorable family with his wife Brittany Mahomes and their two little kids, daughter Sterling Skye, born in February 2021, and son, Patrick "Bronze" Lavon Mahomes III, born in late November 2022.

Patrick and Brittany, who have been together since high school, tied the knot in March 2022.
