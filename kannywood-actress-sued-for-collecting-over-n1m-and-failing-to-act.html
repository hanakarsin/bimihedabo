Kannywood Actress Sued For 'Collecting Over N1m And Failing To Act'

July 03, 2024
Post

A Kannywood actress identified as Hafsat Idris has been sued by a film production firm, UK entertainment, over alleged breach of contract.

UK entertainment in a suit filed before a Kano High Court accused the actress of collecting the sum of ₦1.3 million to feature in a TV series but failed to fulfil the terms of the agreement reportedly signed by both parties.

The film production firm further stated that damages were incurred due to the breach of contract by the actress.

It asked the court to award N10 million as damages, the defendant, however, filed a formal response to the claims through her attorney.

The court adjourned the case for hearing.
