Ugandan Artist, Cindy Sanyu Calls Omah Lay An Idiot In New Video

May 05, 2024
Post

Renowned Ugandan music art, Cinderella Sanyu, better known as Cindy Sanyu has appeared in viral-video clip mocking and condemning Nigerian singer, Omah Lay for celebrating his successful concert in her country.

Naija News reported earlier that Kampala Metropolitan Police arrested Nigerian singers, Omah Lay and Tems in Uganda for flouting COVID-19 guidelines.

The singers were arrested after their performances at The Big Brunch, which held at Speke Resort, Wavamunno Rd, Kampala, Uganda.

Reacting to the development, Sanyu, as seen in the video which has stirred a barrage of reactions on social media laughed Omah Lay and his fellow Nigerians for visiting Ugandan to perform in a concert while artists in the country are starving.
