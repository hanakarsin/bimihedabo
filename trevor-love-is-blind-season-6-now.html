'Love Is Blind' Season 6 Star Trevor Sova Allegedly Had a Girlfriend During Filming

Trevor from 'Love Is Blind' Season 6 is rumored to be on a new Netflix show. Also, did he have had a girlfriend while in the pods?

By Allison DeGrushe

Feb. 23 2024, Updated 4:10 p.m. ET

During Love Is Blind Season 6, we meet Trevor Sova, a hopeful romantic who finds himself head over heels for Chelsea Blackwell. They appear to be a match made in reality TV heaven, but Chelsea seemingly blindsided Trevor by accepting a proposal from Jimmy Presnell.

Fans felt for Trevor as they watched him get dumped on TV. But was he really that good of a guy? Keep reading as we learn more about what Trevor is up to now as well as address rumors that he had a girlfriend during the show.

Where is Trevor from Season 6 of 'Love Is Blind' now?

Since bidding farewell to the world of Love Is Blind, Trevor has settled back into his routine, returning to his job as a project technician for Cardinal Health. A quick peek at his Instagram also reveals he's back in North Carolina, hitting the gym and spending time with his dogs, Bear and Goliath.

Speaking of his furry companions, on Jan. 20, 2024, the reality TV star sadly shared the news of his family dog, Duke's, passing that morning. Trevor said Duke had been dealing with a tumor in his nose, causing breathing problems, which left Trevor's family having to make the heart-wrenching decision to put him to sleep.

Also, if you recall, Trevor told Chelsea in the pods that he had a dog named Chelsea! But on April 8, 2023, he shared the news of her passing via Instagram. He wrote, "Said goodbye to my best friend this week. Thank you for being the most loyal, loving dog I could ever ask for Chels :)."

After Season 6 premiered, he took to Instagram again to talk about Chelsea's death as she had ironically passed away the same day that Chelsea had broke up with him in the pods.

Trevor allegedly had a girlfriend while filming 'Love Is Blind' Season 6

Here's something we didn't have on our Love Is Blind Season 6 bingo card! During filming, Trevor allegedly was dating a woman named Natalia Marrero, per The Reality Ashley.

In a post about their relationship, The Reality Ashley included photos of Natalia and Trevor together and screenshots of text messages they exchanged.

The conversations they had seemingly didn't make sense to a lot of fans though, as in them, Natalia was aware Trevor was going on the show and was seemingly OK with it.

When Trevor's time on the show ended after Chelsea broke up with him, he then appeared to have texted Natalia "Do you still love me?" Then he promised her that when they got home, they'd get married.

But according to The Reality Ashley, Trevor and Natalia broke up two weeks ago.

Trevor is also rumored to be part of the cast of Netflix's 'Perfect Match' Season 2.

Prior to the news breaking that Trevor potentially had a girlfriend on the show, there were also rumors that he was part of the cast of Perfect Match Season 2. The Netflix original series gives singles from other popular reality shows on the platform such as The Ultimatum, Too Hot to Handle, and The Trust, another shot at love.

A premiere date for Perfect Match Season 2 has not yet been announced. But it makes you wonder: if these rumors are true, did Trevor leave his alleged girlfriend again to film another reality TV dating show?

Trevor better pull up at the reunion and do some explaining.
