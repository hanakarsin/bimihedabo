Gospel Singer, Frank Edwards Ends Speculations On Engaging Deborah Paul Enenche

July 09, 2024
Post

Renowned Nigerian gospel singer, Frank Edwards, has taken to his Instagram page to end speculations that he got Deborah Paul Enenche engaged.

Naija News understands that there have been speculations that the Rocktown Records Label Chief Executive Officer (CEO) engaged the daughter of the Senior Pastor of the Dunamis International Gospel Center (DIGC), Dr Paul Enenche, after the young lady took to social media to share pictures of her engagement ring.

Deborah refused to disclose the identity of her lover. However, netizens were quick to link her up with Frank Edwards after a photo she took with the 33-year-old singer surfaced on the internet.

Reacting, however, to the speculations, Frank Edwards took to his Instagram handle on Thursday to declare that the news is not true.

“you all should allow that girl to enjoy her peace with all these fake news”, the singer posted.

Nigerian skit maker, Amarachi Amusi, popular known as Ashmusy on Thursday reacted to the speculations that she is being sponsored by a ‘pot-bellied’ man spotted in her recent social media post.

Naija News reports that this is following her latest acquisition of car and vacation photos and videos which emerged online.

Some Nigerians claimed that the ‘pot-bellied’ man spotted in the videos was her sugar daddy and he is responsible for her luxurious lifestyle.

Reacting to this, Ashmusy in a post shared on her Instagram page rained curses on those attributing her success and achievement to a man.

She said those belittling her hard work and spreading fake news about her will also experience fruitless labour.

She wrote: “Anybody that ever belittles my hustle and attaches my success/achievement to a man will never ever eat the fruit of their labour. He/she will work and not make anything from the work. If you ever decide to give my glory to any man and speak fake news against me…

“If you ever make all my shooting of videos every damn day look like nothing, if you make my running business back to back for 5 years now look like nothing… you will work so hard and not see results.”
