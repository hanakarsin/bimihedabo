Sheikh Muhammad Abdallah has said it is natural for a man to desire more than one woman because all the human male species were created that way.

Abdallah said men are wired to have more than one woman in their lives and they thus can not be satisfied by a single woman.

Speaking on NTV on Saturday, March 20, the sheikh said for women, it is different because they only desire other men when their husbands are not satisfying them.

"It is how we were created. None of us men can be satisfied with one woman. Just check in the society, you will find a man is married but outside that union, he has five to six women, Though the wife will find out and it will be chaos, he said.

In recent days, social media has been awash with the debate on polygamy following Kikuyu musician Samidoh drama with his wife, Edith and baby mama Karen Nyamu.

A recent poll by TUKO.co.ke showed a majority of Kenyans have no problem with polygamy, as long as the man can provide for his many wives, both financially and emotionally.

According to the ladies who were in support of polygamy, the problem with men is doing it discreetly only for the first wife to find out from elsewhere and feel disrespected.
