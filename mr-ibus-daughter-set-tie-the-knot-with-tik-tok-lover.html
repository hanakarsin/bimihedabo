Mr Ibu's Daughter Set Tie The Knot With Tik Tok Lover

March 11, 2024
Post

Daughter of Nigerian actor, John Okafor better known as Mr Ibu, Jasmine, is set to tie the knot with her US based lover.

Naija News understands that Jasmine Okafor in a now-viral video on social media said “Yes” to her lover whom she met on TikTok, two months ago.

Sharing her love story on the video-sharing platform, Jasmine revealed that she met her US-based lover two months ago on the app.

Jasmine, who used her love story to emphasize on how a real man behaves when he is truly in love with you, disclose that her lover flew all the way from the states to propose to her.

According to Jasmine, she would walk down the aisle this month.

She wrote: “We met here on TikTok 2 months ago! He was my TikTok gifter! We are set to get married in this month. He flew all the way from USA to Africa for the first time! Just to marry me. A real man won’t waste your time”.
