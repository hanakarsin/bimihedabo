Mick Norcross - Trivia, Family, Bio

Post

Known for his appearances on the British reality series The Only Way Is Essex. He owned the Sugar Hut club, where he was often featured on The Only Way is Essex.

Before Fame

He debuted on The Only Way is Essex in 2011 during the show's 2nd season. That same year, he appeared on the TV series Loose Women as himself.

Trivia

In 2014, he appeared in 1 episode of the reunion series The Only Way Is Essex: All Back to Essex. He announced his departure from the show in March 2013.

Family Life

He was born and raised in Brentwood, England, where the Sugar Hut nightclub is located. He passed away in January 2021. 

Associated With

He appeared alongside Mark Wright during his time on The Only Way is Essex.
