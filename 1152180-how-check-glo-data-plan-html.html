How to check Glo data plan

Post

Are you a Globacom Nigeria Internet subscriber? Then you should know how to manage your Glo subscription. This post contains extremely useful information on how to check Glo data plan easily from time to time on your phone.

Do not be in the dark about your Glo subscription status. Checking your Glo data plan status and balance from time to time will help you to monitor and manage your Internet data properly. We propose your attention four methods you can use to check your Glo data plan balance:

  • Number one way is via SMS. To receive your Glo data balance and other subscription details, just send INFO to 127. You will get an SMS containing everything you need to know about your current subscription: balance and expiry date.
  • Your second option is to dial *777#. Then you press the number selection that corresponds with your Glo subscription. Select “manage data” and then select “Data Balance”. Then wait patiently for a reply message from Globacom Network.

  • The third method is to dial the Globacom USSD for checking data balance: *127*0#. Information about your data balance and when it will expire will be displayed on your phone screen.
  • And the fourth option is Glo HIS Portal, a self-service portal you can use to manage your Internet plan. Visit http://hsi.glo.com to access the Glo HIS Portal which available only on the Globacom Network. That means you cannot access the website on other mobile and Internet networks.

To know the available data plan for Glo, dial *777# from your Glo number and press the number for plan menu to listen to the plan options. You can also check the available Internet plans on Globacom Nigeria website at www.gloworld.com/ng/personal/data/.

For more information on Glo data plans and other services, you can call customer care number 121 or visit Glo website at www.gloworld.com.
